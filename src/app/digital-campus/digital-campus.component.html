Connecting The Student Community of Your University

The Digital Campus is designed to help your college or university drive student engagement. It supports communication and collaboration between students across Campuses, Faculties, Study levels and Classes.

A BRAND NEW WAY OF ENGAGING STUDENTS

With the Digital Campus you can create new and broader networks between your students, with on the go access to schoolmates, with shared academic personal interests.

STUDENT-CENTERED
The Peersview Digital Campus helps centralize the collective knowledge of the entire school, hereby making the crowd sourcing for knowledge or information more efficient for all students.
INCLUSIVE ACCESS
Instructors and school administrators can interact with students and provide new opportunities for holistic growth.
CO-CURRICULAR NETWORK
Dedicated sections for Clubs and Societies, and An online marketplace for your students.
