This Privacy Policy only applies to information that we collect through the Peersview Online Interface (the "Site," which consists of all content and pages located within the Peersview.com web domain and mobile application).

"Personal Identifiable Information" means any information about yourself that you may provide to us when using the Peersview Service, such as when you sign up for a user account, update or change information for your account, register through or otherwise grant access to a third-party website such as Facebook or Google, subscribe to a Peersview Digital Campus Service, complete a survey, participate in public forums or enter into a transaction through the Site, which may include (but is not limited to) your name, contact information, gender, photo, date of birth, location, billing information, and occupation. We may receive Personally Identifiable Information when you send us an email message or otherwise contact us.

“Non-Personal Information” means information that cannot be used to directly identify you. When users come to our Site, we may track, collect and aggregate Non-Personal Information indicating, among other things, what services users access most often, the order in which they were accessed, when they were accessed, and which hyperlinks were "clicked." We also collect information from the URLs from which you linked to our Site. Collecting such information may involve logging the IP address, operating system and browser software used by each user of the Site session sources, and other data which tracks users' access to the Services. Although such information is not Personally Identifiable Information, we may be able to determine from an IP address a user’s Internet Service Provider and the geographic location of his or her point of connectivity.

Peersview may collect and use any user data you upload to the Peersview network whether it is in the form of opinion, a forum post or other text, image, audio file, audio-visual file, software or any other material uploaded to the Services. When you access our Services by or through a mobile device, we may receive or collect and store unique identification numbers associated with your device, mobile carrier, device type, model and manufacturer, mobile device operating system brand and model, depending on your mobile device settings, your geographical location data. You may be able to change the settings on your computer or mobile device to prevent it from providing us with any location information.

Cookies and other Tracking Technologies

Peersview uses Cookies and other technologies to help personalize and improve the User’s online experience and the overall quality of the Services. Essentially, cookies are a user’s identification card for the Peersview servers. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer or device. Cookies allow Peersview to serve you better and more efficiently. They help us recognize you and to personalize your experience at our Site. Cookies are not used to retain Personally Identifiable Information. From time to time we may also use additional typical methods of collecting data.

You should be able to control how and whether cookies will be accepted by your web browser. Most browsers offer instructions on how to reset the browser to reject cookies in the "Help" section of the toolbar or by choosing the “opt- out” option on cookie pop up notifications. If you reject our cookies, many functions and conveniences of this Site may not work properly.
